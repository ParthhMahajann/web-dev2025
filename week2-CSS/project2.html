EVENT REPORT ON ENGINEERS DAY

Date of the event Held: Engineers Day Celebration

Organizer: 14-09-2024

Venue/Place: Department of ECE in association with Robotic club of CMRTC

Number of students Participants (students and Staff):223

Description about the Event:

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), in collaboration with the Robotic Club, CMRTC, successfully organized the Engineers Day Celebration on 14th September 2024 at Abdul Kalam Seminar Hall, Block-B, CMRTC. The event saw enthusiastic participation from 223 attendees, including students and faculty members. Distinguished guests such as the Director, Deans, and Heads of Departments addressed the gathering, emphasizing the significance of engineers in shaping the future. Various competitions like poster presentation, project display, quiz, and essay writing were held for the students, fostering creativity and technical knowledge. Certificates were awarded to the winners of each competition. Additionally, the outstanding performer award for the academic year 2023-2024 was presented to a faculty member, recognizing their contributions to academics and research. The event successfully inspired students to innovate and reinforced a collaborative spirit within the engineering community.
